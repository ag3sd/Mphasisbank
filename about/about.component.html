Savings Account

� Account balance cannot fall below Rs. 1,000
� Minimum deposit or withdrawal is Rs. 100
� Minimum Rs. 1000 should be deposited at the time of opening the Account

Fixed Deposit Account

� Minimum Deposit is Rs. 10,000
� The rate of compound interest is
      o 6% for up to 1 year
      o 7% for 1 to 2 years
      o 8% for more than 3 years
� Senior citizen customers get 0.5% more interest

Loan Account

� Minimum Loan amount is Rs. 10,000
� The rate of interest is
      o 10% for loans up to 5 lakhs
      o 9.5% for loans from 5 lakhs to 10 lakhs
      o 9% for loans above 10 lakhs
� Senior Citizens cannot be sanctioned a loan of greater than 1 lakh. The rate of interest for Senior citizens is 9.5%
